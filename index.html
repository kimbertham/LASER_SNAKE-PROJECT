The Rules

The aim of the game is to feed you snake the purple grapes and grow your length!

Use your ARROW KEYS to navigate through the map

But be careful, the more you grow the faster you get!

Make sure your snake doesnt eat the poison!

Use your SPACEBAR to shoot your snake lasers and eradicate the poison!

Make sure your snake doesnt crash into the walls and hurt itself!
